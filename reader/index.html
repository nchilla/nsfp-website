An “Intentional Pause”: The Scholar Strike Brings Questions of Justice and Performativity to Campus

On Sep 8 and 9, scholars around the nation striked in protest of the police violence waged against Black Americans. The strike has sparked a dialogue on campus about how to make a sustainable change.
